Brimstone Peak

This data set consists of backscattered electron images of volcanic ash and rock fragment (tephra) layers in exposed bare blue ice areas on Brimstone Peak, Antarctica (75.9°S, 158.5° E). Tephra samples were collected between 15 November 1996 and 15 January 1997.

Please cite these data as follows: Dunbar, N. 2002. Blue Ice Tephra II - Brimstone Peak. Boulder, CO, USA: National Snow and Ice Data Center. http://dx.doi.org/10.7265/N5MG7MDK.
