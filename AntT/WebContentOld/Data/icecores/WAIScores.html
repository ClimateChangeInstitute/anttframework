WAIS Divide

The WAIS Divide deep ice-core drilling site is located at west of the Ross-Amundsen ice-flow divide. The project is part of WAISCORES research project focused on deep ice coring in West Antarctica.

The WAIS Divide deep ice-core drilling started in 2007 and drill reached bedrock at 3,405 meters on December 1, 2011.
